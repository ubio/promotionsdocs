# Glossary

## Validation

The process of testing whether a promotion is valid, invalid, or cannot be validated.

A validation is successful when the system can determine whether the promotion is `VALID` or `INVALID`.

## Validation Result

The top-level outcome of a validation attempt.

## Error Category

A grouped reason explaining why a validation was invalid or could not be completed.

## Reason Code

A specific machine-readable reason for a validation result. Reason codes can apply to `VALID`, `INVALID`, or `COULD NOT VALIDATE`.

## Sitewide

A promotion that applies across the majority or entirety of a merchant's product catalogue.

## SKU-Specific

A promotion that applies only to a specific product, product group, or was validated against a single SKU.
